HOSTEL

The hostels in our campus are designed to provide an ideal environment conducive for academic pursuit. All the hostels are provided with dining halls, uninterrupted water supply, reading halls, round the clock STD, ISD booths, Internet connection, indoor recreation & television.

LIBRARY

Our college has an excellent central library for the benefit of the students and Faculty members. The library is housed in a spacious area of 500 sq.m.,fully computerized with Reference Section, Periodical Section and Stack Area. Presently there are more than 30989 books and 5113 reference volumes stacked for the benefit of staff and students. Besides the central library, each Department has its own library with technical books. The collection of many technical books, management books and Journals provide a salubrious environment for learning.

SPORTS

The aim and objective of the physical education department is to encourage the students to participate in various sports events in order to acquire the physical and psychological benefits.

CANTEEN

College has spacious canteen facility in the college premises and is run by college management. A variety of south indian food and snack items are provided to the students and staff of the college at highly subsidized rates with highest quality.Our college canteen is blessed with family atmosphere, with a variety of delicacies, touched by a home made flavour, cooked to perfection and served with a great courtesy.Refreshments are available at the college canteen at affordable rates. Adequate seating facilities are provided to accomodate students and staff even during peak time.

TRANSPORT

To ensure safe travel the college has its own fleet of outsourced college buses designed as per standards and manned by trained drivers and personnel sensitized to the needs of students. The Transport Service (optional) is operated by the college and a separate transport fee is charged for this. For supervision and monitoring, transport supervisor is on board throughout the journey.

LABS

Every Department has well organized laboratories fitted with state-of-the-art equipments for providing hands-on practical experience to the students.
